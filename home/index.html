What do we do?

Our main goal is to provide web presence and technology to all schools for free. We do this by following the three segments on the right. At SMZ Education, we create, maintain, and update a website, emails, and Google Maps data for your school!

Learn More
Website

SMZ Education provides your school with a free website that goes by the domain "yourschool".smzedu.com. This domain gives your school a professional website with all the features you need.

E-Mail

SMZ Education makes email addresses available to your school that go along: "yourschool"@smzedu.com. We modify as many emails as your school needs for you and your colleagues.

Help

SMZ Education hosts all the websites on our side, meaning you don't have any hassle! If you want to modify parts of your website; you can contact us at support@smzedu.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do we need to pay anything?

No — the core services provided by SMZ Education (website creation, basic hosting, Google Maps entries and school email setup) are offered free of charge to eligible schools. We rely on donations and volunteers to cover hosting, maintenance, and development costs. Donations are optional but help us expand and maintain the service, provide faster support, and fund feature improvements. If a school requests custom development, premium integrations, or extended support beyond our standard offering, we may provide paid options or help the school find an affordable third-party provider.

What are the requirements?

To apply, you should represent an educational institution (school, preschool, learning centre, etc.). We ask for basic information such as the school's name, location, an authorized contact person, and proof of affiliation (for example an official email or document) where applicable. The school should have someone who can act as the day-to-day contact for content updates and approvals. A stable internet connection on the school's side is helpful but not strictly required for all services. After an application, our team will review and reach out to guide you through the onboarding steps.

How do I un-enroll?

If you decide to stop using our services, please email support@smzedu.com with your school name, domain (if assigned), and a brief reason for un-enrolling. We'll confirm the account ownership, provide a short checklist to help you back up any data (website files, email contents if applicable), and schedule the removal. Typical removal takes a few business days; we will send a confirmation once the school has been fully removed from our systems. If you need assistance migrating data before un-enrolling, our team can advise on options.

Why is your service free?

SMZ Education is a non-profit initiative funded primarily by donations and supported by volunteers. Offering free core services aligns with our mission to increase educational access. Donations cover hosting, domain registration, platform maintenance, and development of new tools. We also partner with organizations and volunteers who contribute time and expertise. Keeping services free ensures smaller or underfunded schools can still access a professional online presence and basic digital tools. If you are an independent person and would like to support us, you can donate!

Which countries are eligible?

We accept applications from schools worldwide. In some cases we may need to comply with local laws, sanctions, or contractual restrictions; if so, we will inform applicants during review. Our primary considerations are the school's educational purpose and the ability to communicate with an authorized contact. If you're unsure about eligibility in your country, reach out to contact@smzedu.com and we'll provide guidance.

How can I share SMZ Education?

Sharing our work helps us grow. You can: share your school's SMZ Education site link on social media; write a short testimonial or case study we can feature; introduce other schools or local organisations that might benefit; and support us by donating or spreading our fundraising links. For partnerships, guest posts, or social campaigns, email contact@smzedu.com and we'll coordinate.
